# Talking Points

Concise talking points for discussing POS assessment, AI governance, BRD reformatting, and delivery feasibility.

## Positioning

> “I would approach this with an AI Transformation Pathfinder lens: understand the current POS / sales platform journey and product rules, while also checking delivery feasibility, system dependency, and responsible AI controls.”

## Safe Project Framing

- Known area: current POS / sales platform, sales journey AI, BRD / template reformat.
- Working assumption: current POS / sales platform may be the front-office sales platform, but actual scope should be confirmed.
- Open question: What is the count unit, target export format, and expected AI output?

## Meeting Lines

- “Can we confirm the current POS / sales platform scope first?”
- “Is this pain point mainly process, system, data, vendor, or control related?”
- “Do we know whether this rule is configured in the POS platform or handled manually?”
- “What evidence supports this pain point: NIGO data, cycle time, user feedback, or sample cases?”
- “Should we treat this as a quick improvement, vendor enhancement, or longer-term transformation item?”

## Strong Close

> “To make the discussion actionable, I suggest documenting each opportunity by journey step, pain point, data dependency, control requirement, metric, and next decision.”
