# Project Alignment Questions

Questions for aligning with a project lead on priorities, expected outputs, stakeholders, and next-step preparation.

## Opening

> “I would like to prepare with BA, PM, and FDE lenses. Could we align on where I should focus next?”

## Scope / Priority

- What should the next output be: question list, current-state summary, deck content, BRD tracker, or meeting notes?
- Is the priority POS assessment, AI use cases, BRD reformat, or product configuration?
- Which stakeholders are most relevant: business, IT, vendor, compliance, operations, or sales?
- What context should be understood before the next internal or client meeting?

## Current POS / Sales Platform

- Which sales journey steps matter most for this workstream?
- Are there known POS pain points or hypotheses?
- Are there existing materials: screenshots, process maps, issue logs, BRDs, NIGO data?
- Are product rules mainly configured in POS, downstream systems, or manual processes?

## AI Transformation

- Are we proposing new AI use cases or assessing existing ideas?
- Should AI scope focus on agent-facing, operations-facing, product-team-facing, or customer-facing use cases?
- Which AI topics are sensitive from a compliance or client perspective?
- Which governance lens matters most: privacy, suitability, auditability, model risk, or all?

## BRD / Template

- What is included in the batch of legacy templates/items?
- Is the task document reformatting, rule extraction, tracker creation, or export mapping?
- Who validates the reformatted requirements?
- Do we need source traceability?
