# Discovery Questions

Stakeholder-based discovery questions for clarifying scope, pain points, system touchpoints, data readiness, and risk/control considerations.

## Scope

- Which market, channel, and product lines are in scope?
- What does the current POS / sales platform include?
- What decision should this assessment support?
- Are we expected to assess, recommend, prototype, or prepare implementation?
- What is included in the batch of legacy templates/items?
- What does export mean: Excel, CSV, BRD template, system import, or tracker?

## Business / Product Owner

- Which product rules change most often?
- Which rules are ambiguous or manually interpreted?
- Who approves product rule changes?
- Are BRDs used for documentation only, or do they affect POS configuration?

## Sales / Channel

- Where do agents spend the most time?
- Which steps cause customer confusion?
- Where do agents use workarounds such as Excel, email, or manual notes?
- Which POS step would the sales team improve first?

## Operations / Underwriting

- What are the top NIGO or referral reasons?
- Which supporting documents are most often missing or incorrect?
- Which checks happen too late?
- What case information is missing at underwriting handoff?

## IT / POS / Vendor

- Which rules are configurable in the current POS / sales platform?
- Which changes require vendor support?
- What integrations exist with CRM, underwriting, policy admin, payment, or document systems?
- Can journey data, case data, and document status be extracted for reporting or AI use?
- What audit logs, role-based access, and retention controls exist?

## Compliance / Risk

- Which journey steps require suitability, disclosure, consent, or approval evidence?
- Which AI use cases need compliance / legal / risk review?
- What customer, health, or financial data restrictions apply?
- What must be logged if AI supports agent or operations work?
